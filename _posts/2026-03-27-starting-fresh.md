I wanted a place to write without negotiating with a dashboard every time an idea showed up.

<!--more-->

So this site is simpler now.

Each post is just a Markdown file in the repository. If I have something to say, I write it, push it, and it goes live. No extra platform, no editor lock-in, no feeling that the writing belongs to someone else's product.

That also means the blog can stay small on purpose. I want room for research notes, language thoughts, and the occasional personal essay that would feel strange anywhere else.

If you are reading this, the reset worked.
