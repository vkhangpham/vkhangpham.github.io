in a Souls game, a **bonfire** is where you can rest, level up, and plan your journey.

<!--more-->

bonfires are also landmarks, signaling a beginning, or an ending, of a region, an act, or an important event.

I am heavily influenced by Souls games, especially **Elden Ring**. The revolution in FromSoft games doesn't just come from their concepts, but also their crazy good execution. But enough about the games, there will definitely be a post about them.

so basically, the blog's name, <img src="/assets/emotes/bonfire.webp" alt="bonfire" class="inline-emote"> _between bonfires_ <img src="/assets/emotes/bonfire.webp" alt="bonfire" class="inline-emote">, is a poetic way of saying I am **side-questing** to strengthen up before milestones of my life.

there might not be many important milestones at all, or there might be no reflection point before a big milestone, but that's just how life works, I guess. So I am very happy right now to have the time and enjoyment to write this blog.

after all, it takes FromSoft so many years to realize they should put a bonfire before _every_ boss fight, so yeah, good luck to me.
