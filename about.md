I care a lot about languages, machine learning, research, and the strange human side of technology. a bonfire is where I can write without squeezing every thought into a social feed or a product dashboard.

You should expect a mix of:

- software notes
- language-learning reflections
- research thoughts
- essays that are mostly me trying to understand my own thinking
